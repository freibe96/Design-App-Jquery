When is EAA?

July 22nd through July 28th 2019

Where is EAA?

EAA Aviation Center, 300 Poberezny Road Oshkosh, WI 54902

What is EAA?

EAA is the largest annual garthering of aviation enthusiasts in the world. It offers everything from unique, historic aircrafts to world-class daily air shows. Trust us, you don't want to miss the World's Greatest Aviation Celebration. If you are interested in attending EAA you can purchase #OSH19 tickets here!
